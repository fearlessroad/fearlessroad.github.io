New publication simulating sea spray aerosols!
See the full manuscript in ACS Central Science entitled Revealing the Impacts of Chemical Complexity on Submicrometer Sea Spray Aerosol Morphology. Here we utilize large-scale all-atom molecular dynamics to simulate organic sea spray aerosols! I'm also happy to report that my artwork was selected for the cover of this journal issue!
