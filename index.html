May 4, 2023

New Publication Alert!

Our work simulating sea spray aerosols is finally out in ACS Central Science! The full publication, entitled Revealing the Impacts of Chemical Complexity on Submicrometer Sea Spray Aerosol Morphology, can be accessed online here. We used large-scale all-atom molecular dynamics to simulate whole organic sea spray aerosols to understand their dynamics and climate-relevant properties! I'm also happy to report that my artwork was selected for the cover of this journal issue!
